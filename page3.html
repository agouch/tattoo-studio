About the Artist

Booking About

Aliyah is a software engineering student who possesses a unique passion for art and creativity. Her journey has led her to the world of tattooing, where she has flourished as an artist. Aliyah's tattoos are a testament to her meticulous nature, characterized by intricate details, heavy shading, and bold, thick lines. Her work beautifully melds elements of traditional American tattoo styles with a touch of realism, resulting in captivating and distinctive designs that leave a lasting impression. Aliyah's ability to merge her technical skills with her artistic flair is a true testament to her multifaceted talents, making her a remarkable and sought-after tattoo artist.
